Artist Statement

This interactive poem turns my CCD recipes into draggable puzzle pieces. Each recipe represents a different emotional flavor: breakfast confidence, bakery sweetness, chocolate comfort, coffee energy, or savory protection.

The project is inspired by Berry Vibes Studio because it feels like a playful app where cozy food, design, and personal growth battle together. The reader has to move the poem by hand, so the act of dragging becomes part of the meaning.
